Institution of Electric and Electronic Engineers (IEEE)

As Industrial Relations Officer, I contact recruiters from various companies to organize info sessions and workshops for the students at UC Berkeley. Additionally, I help organize the annual Startup Career Fair.

Pocket: iOS Application

My friends and I created a budgeting application geared towards young adults to ease their transition into economic independence. We provided a clean user interface for the user to keep track of their finances. I worked on the Analysis Page which displayed charts and graphs that would update with every user input.

Project RISHI

Project RISHI is a 100% student run non profit that promotes sustainable development in rural communities in India. As the Director of Fundraising, I organize fundraisers throughout the year and cultivate relationships with our donors. I am also in charge of the biannual crowdfunding campaign.

UC Berkeley South Indian Society

As Director of Technology, I, individually, created the main website from scratch for the club. Additonally, I also manage the audio and visual responsibilities of the club.

CS KickStart

After attending the week-long CS KickStart program at UC Berkeley, that offers instruction in Python and HTML, I joined the CS KickStart Planning Commitee. As part of the Industrial Relations Committee, I reach out to companies for potential sponsorships, company visits and guest speakers.

Saint Francis High School

I graduated in May 2016 with Magna Cum Laude Honors. At Saint Francis High School, I was on the Cross Country Team, was Treasurer of the Interact Club, and served the California State Federation Board.
